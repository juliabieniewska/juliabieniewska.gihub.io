I am Julia, a physics PhD student moving to Data Science

Knowledgeable, friendly, dedicated, and really into data science. I am a PhD student at the Centre for Cold Matter at Imperial College London. One day, analysing the data from my experiment I realised that it's my favourite part of the job. Then, I went on to learn a number of techniques in machine and deep learning and enjoyed it so much that Data Science became my career of choice. In my free time I used publicly available datasets to gain interesting insights and try out different machine learning concepts, which are now presented in this portfolio.
